## Sales Performance Executive Summary

**Regional Revenue Drivers:** The South region is the primary revenue driver, contributing ~36% of total revenue ($697K) with 564 orders. East follows at 23% ($455K), while North and West contribute roughly 20-21% each. The South's dominance suggests strong market penetration or higher average order values in that region.

**Category Revenue Drivers:** Electronics is the clear category leader, generating ~32% of revenue ($617K) despite having fewer orders than Apparel and Beauty categories (472 vs 408/395 orders). This indicates Electronics commands a significantly higher average order value (~$1,300/order vs ~$800-900 for other categories). The remaining four categories are tightly clustered around $330K each.

**Overall Trend:** Monthly revenue has remained relatively stable between $60K-$110K from July 2024 through September 2025, with modest growth (4%-16% MoM) and occasional dips (-19%). This baseline suggests consistent, predictable business operations during the first three quarters.

**Critical Anomaly:** November 2025 represents a major anomaly—a dramatic spike to $397K (+262% MoM) followed by an equally sharp drop in December ($142K, -64% MoM). This volatility is inconsistent with historical patterns and suggests either: (1) data quality issues like duplicate entries, (2) a one-time promotional event that wasn't sustained, or (3) a system anomaly.

**Recommendations for Next Quarter:**
1. **Immediate investigation** of November 2025 transactions to determine if they represent real business activity or need correction/exclusion from baseline projections.
2. **Focus on Electronics category optimization**—given its high revenue contribution and premium pricing, explore ways to maintain or grow this segment.
3. **Expand South region initiatives** while monitoring East for potential growth opportunities given its solid 23% share.
4. **Establish anomaly detection protocols** to catch similar spikes early in future reporting cycles.

**Electronics is the clear leader**, generating approximately **620,000**, while all other categories are clustered around **330,000-340,000**:
- Electronics: ~620,000 (dominant)

### Critical Anomaly to Investigate
